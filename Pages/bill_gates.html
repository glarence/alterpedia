One of the richest people in history and today, Bill Gates has amassed enormous wealth from cofounding Microsoft, one of the world's largest PC software companies.

Gates changed the computing world: how people and businesses used computers.

Despite his affluence, Bill Gates is one of the most charitable people, with his foundation donating lots of money to helping the world.

What Could've Been

Without the CEO, chairman, and chief software architect, Microsoft would have never existed

  • No Windows, which 91% (2013) of computers run, personal to business
  • No monopoly of the business market, where Microsoft reigns for office and OS software
  • There would be no Apple, another large computing company that would have shutdown without Microsoft's $150 million investment in 1997

We would be farther from saving the world without "The Giving Pledge", a campaign founded by Bill Gates and Warren Buffett where pledges give away at least half of their wealth, which translates to at least 40 billion dollars from Bill Gates alone

We would be missing even more funds for charitable causes without Gates' foundation, which has raised at least $60 billion
