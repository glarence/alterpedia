Christopher Nolan is an English-American film director, screenwriter, and producer.

His nine films have grossed over US$4.2 billion worldwide and garnered a total of 26 Oscar nominations and seven awards.

Gaining acclaim for some of his independent works, he would then direct some of the biggest, most popular blockbusters in the past decade.

What Could've Been

We would be without "The Dark Knight", "Inception", and "Interstellar", a few of the most groundbreaking, unique films in the past decade that has had some of the most discussion

The use of misdirection may not be as popular as it is today without Nolan, one of the masters of misdirection

There would be less efforts to preserving cherished filmmaking techniques, like using 35 mm film as Nolan has tried to save from becoming a dying art
