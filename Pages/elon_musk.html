Elon Musk has helped found some of the most innovative companies in the past decade or two: SpaceX, Zip2, PayPal, and Tesla Motors.

Characterized by a notable executive as "Wernher von Braun, Howard Hughes, Henry Ford, Bill Gates, Steve Jobs, and every other badass, all rolled into one.", Musk is a truly special, intellectual individual who's hellbent on saving humanity.

He may be the man who solves the world's energy crisis and/or takes us to Mars before we know it.

What Could've Been

The automative industry would've remained unchallenged and pollution may remain untackled without Musk's efforts in creating Tesla leading to the most innovative electric cars ever, which combine environmental benefits, performance, and beauty in one

Online and P2P payments may not have come to fruition without Paypal, a company Elon Musk had his hands in early on

Space expedition probably would have stayed stagnant, without SpaceX's efforts in creating affordable, reusable rockets, thanks to Elon

There would be no competition for creating advanced interregional transportation without Elon Musk's Hyperloop proposal, a high-speed system that can travel between Los Angeles and San Francisco in around 35 minutes
