John Oliver is an English comedian, political commentator, television host, and occasional actor.

He's most notably known for hosting HBO's "Last Week Tonight with John Oliver" and his work on "The Daily Show with Jon Stewart".

Oliver has had tremendous impact with his political voice in the US, advocating moral causes and highlighting unknown and well known issues with a cheeky perspective.

What Could've Been

Without John Oliver, there would be no "John Oliver Effect": his comedic commentary impacting US politics

  • His encouragement for people to comment on the FCC's website regarding net neutrality crashed the site
  • Congress credits him in helping win a vote to enforce protections for chicken farmers fighting immoral industry practices
  • Led to 25,000 in donations after highlighting Miss America Organization and Foundation failing to back up claims of distributing scholarship money
  • Received mountains of donations later given to Doctors Without Borders after creating a church in order to highlight tax exemption issues

There may be no Community or The Daily Show, where John Oliver did a lot of great work as a fellow writer, correspodent, and actor
