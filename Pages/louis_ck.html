Louis C.K. is an American comedian, actor, writer, producer, director, and editor.

He's most known for his standup specials and the creator, star, writer, director, executive producer, and primary editor of the acclaimed comedy-drama series, Louie.

Louis C.K.'s use of observational, self-deprecating, dark and vulgar humor in his brutally honest segments has elevated him to being one of the best stand up comics of all time.
