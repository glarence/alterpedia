Michael Jackson was an American singer, songwriter, record producer, dancer, and actor.

Called the King of Pop, his contributions to music and dance, along with his publicized personal life, made him a global figure in popular culture for over four decades.

His distinctive sound and style has influenced numerous artists of various music genres.

Jackson has won hundreds of awards, making him the most awarded recording artist in the history of popular music.

What Could've Been

Some of today's artists, like Usher, Chris Brown, or Justin Timberlake, may have never made it without Michael Jackson, one whom many cited as an influence

We would be without some of the greatest songs of all time, like "Thriller", "Bad", "Beat It", and "Billie Jean"

Many Black artists would not have better opportunities without Jackson, much of his work helping break down racial barriers

Moves like the "robot" or the "moonwalk" would not be as popular or well known, which have been emulated by many people
