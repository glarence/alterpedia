Oprah Winfrey is an American media proprietor, talk show host, actress, producer, and philanthropist.

Dubbed the "Queen of All Media",[6] she has been ranked the richest African-American of the 20th century, the greatest black philanthropist in American history, and is currently (2015) North America's only black billionaire.

Several assessments regard her as the most influential woman in the world.
