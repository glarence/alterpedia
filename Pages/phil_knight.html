Phil Knight is an American business magnate.

A native of Oregon, he is the co-founder and chairman of Nike, Inc., and previously served as CEO of the company.

In November 2015, Forbes named Knight the 15th richest person in the world, with an estimated net worth of US$28.1 billion.

Phil Knight in his time at Nike has revolutionized the sports and marketing industry with eye catching appeal and forward thinking design/technology
