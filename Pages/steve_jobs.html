Steve Jobswas an American technology entrepreneur, visionary and inventor.

He was the co-founder, chairman, and chief executive officer (CEO) of Apple Inc.; CEO and largest shareholder of Pixar Animation Studios; a member of The Walt Disney Company's board of directors following its acquisition of Pixar; and founder, chairman, and CEO of NeXT Inc.

Jobs is widely recognized as a pioneer of the microcomputer revolution of the 1970s, along with Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak.

What Could've Been

The smartphone industry would not be as big or impactful as it is now without the release of the first iPhone, a product Steve Jobs had a huge hand in

Perhaps minimalist, beautiful yet functional design may not be as profound as it is now, something Steve Jobs pushed and Apple was known for

We would be missing some of our favorite animated movies like Toy Story, The Incredibles, or Inside Out without Pixar, which Steve Jobs cofounded
