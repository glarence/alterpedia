Taylor Swift is an American singer-songwriter and actress.

Swift is known for narrative songs about her personal experiences. As a songwriter, she has been honored by the Nashville Songwriters Association and the Songwriters Hall of Fame.

Swift has also had supporting roles in feature films including Valentine's Day (2010) and The Giver (2014).
